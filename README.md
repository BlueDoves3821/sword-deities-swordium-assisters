## It's Time To Assist!
This add-on adds the swordium assister factory to the game.
<p align="center">
  <img src="sprites/blocks/production/swordium-assister-factory.png" width="100">
</p>
And depending on what material you use, it'll produce 8 types of assisters that give 5 minutes of status effects.
<p align="center">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-darkheartium.png" width="75">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-firebrandium.png" width="75">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-ghostwalkerium.png" width="75">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-icedaggerium.png" width="75">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-illuminaium.png" width="75">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-swordium.png" width="75">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-venomshankium.png" width="75">
  <img src="sprites/units/swordium-assister-windforceium.png" width="75">
</p>
From being a slow-moving tank with the venomshank's tanker.
<p align="center">
  <img src="sprites/status/venomshanks-tanker.png" width="100">
</p>
To having 6x movement speed, but have the equalivent of 10 burning status stacked on top of itselves with the firebrand's burnspeed.
<p align="center">
  <img src="sprites/status/firebrands-burnspeed.png" width="100">
</p>
And finally, to have an extremely short reload speed with windforce's quickshot.
<p align="center">
  <img src="sprites/status/windforces-quickshot.png" width="100">
</p>
You'll be decimating enemies in no time!
